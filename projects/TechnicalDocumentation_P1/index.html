Introduction

The Binding of Isaac is an Action-Roguelike indie game created by Edmund McMillen.It was originally presented as a small project made for fun, but it became so popular that it became a huge franchise, it became into one of if not the most popular indie game of it's genre.

The game itself is about trasversing diferent sets of stages, collecting various items on the journey making your Character stronger in order to defeat the final boss, of course the boss you fight depends on the path you choose to follow.

As of today, there have been a total of 5 expansions of the game, each adding a fair amount of new content. These are as follows:

  • TBOI: Wrath of the Lamb
  • TBOI: Rebirth
  • TBOI: Afterbirth
  • TBOI: Aterbirth +
  • TBOI: Repentance

Items

There are over a thousand items and trinkets in the latest version of the game, we won't be going over every single one of course but here'es a general explanation of how the item system works:

Passive items are the most common kind in the game, their function is simple: You grab the item and then that item's effect modifies your attack pattern, stats, abilities, size or it applies you a special effect permanently.

Active items function on a different way: You grab the item, it only activates when you press the spacebar key, it may only be used when its charged and its effects usually only last for a single room.

Trinkets are maybe the most easily explained: You grab the trinket and aquire its effect permanently until you decide to drop by holding the Ctrl key.

Debug Console

If you are interested in playing the game in an easy way, you can enable de debug console.

To do this, you must change the data contained in the config.ini file. You must change the command

EnableDebugConsole = 0

to

EnableDebugConsole = 1

Now you are ready to use the debug console in any way yu want, we will show you a few things you will be capable of doing with this new tool.

You can give yourself any item you want by typing "g" or "give" followed by cItemId, like this:

give c001

You can spawn any item or entitie by typing "spawn" followed by the object id:

spawn 2.100.001

And finally, you can teleport to any level you want by typing "floor" followed by the floor id, such as:

floor 2a
